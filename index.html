What You Can Share

Photo + caption

The baseline. A moment, a context, a story. Works on its own — no audio needed.

+ Audio clip

Attach an MP3 or WAV to bring the moment to life. The section, the solo, the take. Let people hear it.

Audio only

Skip the photo entirely. Just the sound. A voice memo, a rough beat, a texture — pure audio moment.
